Many vegans are in fact health conscious and understand the benefits of following such eating patterns. The Vegan diet can be defined as the exclusion of all animal derived foods including meat, poultry, fish, eggs and dairy products from consumption and instead is emphasized on plant foods, grains, legumes, nuts, seeds and vegetable oils. Vegans have become widely accepted and can potentially have many health benefits by minimizing obesity, regulating blood pressure, reducing the risk of chronic diseases such as cardiovascular disease, cancer and aids in the prevention of type 2 diabetes.
